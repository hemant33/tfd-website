FAQ's

1) Who can register on it?

A trader, broker, procurement company, logistics company, farmer can register on it.

2) How do you operate?

We have an android mobile application named as tradeforsure.

3) How do you help traders/brokers?

A trader/broker can post their buy/sell requirement through the application. Interested users will send you an enquiry, out of which any enquiry can be selected. After selecting the enquiry, a contract over mutual agreement can be made between buyer and supplier.

4) Do I always have to post my requirements on the application?

No. You can also call us on our contact number and ask us to complete your requirement.

5) What if I am not sure about the quality of the commodities posted by the supplier?

You should create a mutual agreement with buyer for providing advance payment. Trade for sure helps to keep advance payment from buyer with a third party Nodal account. In case the buyer reneges, advance amount will be transferred to the supplier.

6) What do you do about logistics?

Trade for sure provides logistics companies with the trade deals. Thus logistics companies can bid over the trade deal. A trader/broker can choose the best logistics bid to complete his trade.

7) How do you help Procurement Companies?

Procurement companies can post their requirement on Trade for sure application. Procurement companies can choose selective Traders/Brokers registered on the application who can bid on their requirement. Thus procurement company can choose the best bid and complete the transaction.
